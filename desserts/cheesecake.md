# Cacke recipe
This recipe makes one cheesecake, perfect for a small group.
You can frost it or just dust it with powdered sugar.


## Preparation
- https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/white-chocolate-cheesecake

## Ingredients
- 150g unsalted butter melted, plus extra to grease
- 400g white chocolate broken into pieces
- 300g full-fat cream cheese (we used Philadelphia)
- 250g mascarpone
- 300ml double cream
- 200g strawberries or raspberries, to serve
